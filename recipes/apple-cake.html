Apple Cake

Description

This French apple cake is packed with apples and has a dense, creamy base with a cake-like top layer. It's the perfect cake to enjoy with a cup of coffee or tea in the afternoon.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 3 apples - peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl.
  3. Beat eggs, 1 cup sugar, vanilla extract, and almond extract together in a bowl using an electric mixer until smooth and creamy. Beat in flour mixture and melted butter until batter is smooth; pour into the prepared springform pan.
  4. Arrange apple slices atop batter, overlapping each, in a ring pattern. Mix 1/4 cup sugar and cinnamon together in a bowl; sprinkle over apples.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until cake is set in the middle, 40 to 50 minutes. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing ring from springform pan.
